Research & Development

Our R&D Pipeline

We invest significantly in research and development to bring new treatments to patients. Our pipeline includes:

  1. Discovery Phase: Target identification and validation
  2. Preclinical Development: Laboratory and animal testing
  3. Clinical Trials: Phase I, II, and III human studies
  4. Regulatory Submission: Documentation for approval
  5. Post-Market Surveillance: Ongoing safety monitoring
